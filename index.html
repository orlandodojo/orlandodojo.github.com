Welcome to the Orlando Dojo coding dojo group!

What is the Orlando Dojo?

We are a group of programmers and technology enthusiasts interested in improving our craft.

What happens at a meeting?

  1. We pick a programming language.
  2. We pick an interesting problem to work on.
  3. We take turns, two people at a time, pair programming on the problem in the language we chose.
  4. We take a look at what worked well and what could be improved.

How often do you meet?

We're currently meeting on a biweekly schedule. Normally dojo's are held on a Saturday, though if enough interested people want to have a dojo on another day, we can discuss it and possible make it happen. It's happened before :).

Do I need to be a programmer to participate?

No, all that is needed is an interest and willingness to learn.
